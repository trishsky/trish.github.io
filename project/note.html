Discriminations in Danamku on Videos Hosting Website

Objective

To catch a glimpse on how discrimination is carried out in the context of Danmaku. To raise the awareness of the issue and try to start a discussion.

Methodology

Hate speech has been a problem for a long time on China’s cybersphere. Discriminatory and abusing comments towards ethnic minorities, people from particular regions, of particular gender can usually be found on BBS and commenting platform on the Internet. In this mini-research and the related reporting, discrimination in a rather new and special form of comment, “Danmaku” is studied.

“Danmaku”

The Japanese term “Danmaku” had been used to describe a barrage of bullets appears on the screen or layout of animes, comics and video games.

The term was later borrowed to represent the real-time overlaying comment on the video players of video hosting websites, which first appeared on nicovideo.jp in 2006.

According to a research named “Understanding Gratifications of Watching Danmaku Videos by — Videos with Overlaid Comments” by Yue Chen, Qin GaoPei-Luen Patrick Rau, Danmaku comments is of a more synchronic manner and it can provide a special way of entertainment and information retrieval. It also satisfies needs for company and belonging for the viewers. Moreover, the real-time commenting can also be used to automatically detect key tags at a particular time points of the video.

Despite having a less significant share of video hosting website market in China, Bilibili.com is chosen to be the source of Danmaku because it is has a very active atmosphere of the real-time video overlaying commenting.

Groups studied in this mini research:

The selection of the groups being discriminated in the text of Danamku to studied in this research is based on the notice from Bilibili.com about preventing “comparison” that leads to a more superior or inferior images of particular groups or individuals:

“引战：比较」这种形式往往会带来争吵，我们禁止一切为了贬低或提高一个内容，而引入另一个比较对象的行为。「比较」包括但不限于民族、种族、国家、地域、宗教、性别、年龄、身份、兴趣等。例：「**人就是**」、「*太垃圾」”

The following groups was studied:

  • Ethnic minorities from Xinjiang (keywords: 维吾尔族、清真、新疆、穆斯林)
  • People encountering regional discrimination -- people from Henan province (keyword: 河南人)
  • African (keyword: 黑人)
  • Gender: Female(keyword: 女性)
  • Gender: LGBTQ group: homosexuality (keyword: 同性恋)

Webscraping

With Python script, the Danmaku of the videos (first 100 videos from from the search results, except for videos titled "people from Henan province", only Danmaku from 50 videos were downloaded) titled the aforementioned keywords was downloaded automatically.
